history_edu
Origin Story

Taught by Jesus to his disciples during the Sermon on the Mount (Matthew 6:9-13) and in the Gospel of Luke (11:2-4). It is the quintessential Christian prayer, serving as a model for all prayer.

volunteer_activism
How to Pray

Pray this with deep reverence, pausing after each petition to reflect on its meaning. It is traditionally recited during Mass after the Canon and before Communion.
