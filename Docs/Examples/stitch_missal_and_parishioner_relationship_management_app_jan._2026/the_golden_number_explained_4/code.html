The Golden Number

The Golden Number indicates the position of a specific year within the 19-year Metonic cycle. In this ancient cycle, the phases of the moon recur on the exact same calendar days.

The Formula

(Year % 19) + 1

Example: For 2024, the Golden Number is 11.
